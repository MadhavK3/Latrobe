02 // Digital Twin Solution

A Virtual Replica of the City

We create a living digital model where every road, footpath, dain, and junction is assigned a unique digital identity. This creates a single source of truth for urban management.

  • Asset ID Generation: Unique hash for every infrastructure element.
  • Metadata Storage: Construction date, material type, contractor details, warranty period.
  • Lifecycle Tracking: Complete history of repairs and inspections.
